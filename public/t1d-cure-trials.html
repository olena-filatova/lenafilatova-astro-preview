Educational tracker — not medical advice and not a recommendation to join any trial. Trial phases, eligibility, locations and results change often; always confirm the current details on the study's official page before acting, and discuss any trial with your own diabetes team. "Estimated availability" is a rough, best-case guess if a therapy keeps succeeding — most experimental treatments never reach the market, and timelines slip. Primary source for each study is ClinicalTrials.gov (search the NCT number); some also list on the EU trials register or the sponsor's site.
