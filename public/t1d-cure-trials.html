Educational tracker — not medical advice and not a recommendation to join any study. Phases, eligibility, locations, cost and results change often; always confirm the current details on the study's official page before acting, and discuss any study with your own diabetes team. "Estimated availability" is a rough, best-case guess if a therapy keeps succeeding — most experimental treatments never reach the market, and timelines slip. "Free to join" means taking part doesn't cost you (the study covers its treatment); it doesn't include your own travel unless stated. Primary source for each study is ClinicalTrials.gov (search the NCT number); some also list on the EU trials register, ISRCTN or the sponsor's site.
